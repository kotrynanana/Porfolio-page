About the project

Lekėčiai, a small town surrounded by lush forests, is my hometown and home to the Mėlynių Festivalis. This annual event celebrates the humble blueberry, a berry locals have harvested for centuries. For me, picking blueberries as a child was my first experience with earning money.

The festival's atmosphere is cozy and traditional, with homemade blueberry pies, handcrafted products, and a lively concert bringing the community together. To capture this essence, I designed a hand-drawn, cozy logo that feels like a homemade pie, featuring a stylized blueberry accent. Versatility was key — this logo is used on everything from small stickers and cups to large banners, flags, and diplomas. It is scalable, editable, and can be adapted for black-and-white formats, ensuring it fits seamlessly across all festival materials.

Design Process

  • The logo needed to evoke a cozy, homemade feel, like a homemade pie.
  • Included a stylized blueberry accent for versatility.
  • Designed to be used on various materials, including stickers, cups, diplomas, medals, banners, and posters.
  • Scalable and editable for black-and-white versions.
  • The blueberry accent ties the design to the festival's theme and heritage.

Inspiration

Handwritten blueberry pie recipe, wild blueberries, and fresh, warm blueberry pie.
