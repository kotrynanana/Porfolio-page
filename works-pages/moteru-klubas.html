About the project

The Lekėčių Bendruomenės Moterų Klubas is a community-focused women's club in Lekėčiai, Lithuania, dedicated to celebrating and preserving local traditions. The objective of this project was to design a logo that honors these cultural values.

The logo needed to represent the club's deep connection to Lithuanian traditions and history. It had to encapsulate the essence of their heritage while remaining versatile for various uses.

Design Process

  • Researched traditional Lithuanian symbols to capture the club's essence.
  • Chose the tulip flower, an important motif in Lithuanian art and culture.
  • Explored different arrangements, ultimately selecting a trio of tulips symbolizing unity and tradition.
  • The final logo features three ethnic Lithuanian tulip flowers.
  • Symbolizes the club's dedication to both tradition and holistic living.
  • The color palette and design elements evoke warmth, nature, and community.

Inspiration

Tulips used in Lithuanian folk patterns
