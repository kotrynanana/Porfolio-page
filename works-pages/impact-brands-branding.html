- 2023 -

About the project

The IMPACT BRANDS brand book represents a transformative step in our company's visual identity. This comprehensive guide was designed to provide a cohesive and professional brand presence across all touchpoints.

With a focus on clarity and consistency, the brand book includes detailed guidelines for logo usage, color schemes, and a complete suite of branded elements. This initiative aims to enhance brand recognition, streamline communication, and ensure a unified visual style for IMPACT BRANDS.

Design Process

  • Research and Analysis

    Conducted a thorough analysis of existing brand materials and visual identity to identify areas for improvement. Researched industry trends and best practices to inform design decisions.

  • Logo and Color Usage

    Developed clear and precise guidelines for logo application and color usage, ensuring that all branding elements align with the company's vision and values.

  • Elements Creation

    Designed a comprehensive suite of branded materials, including business cards, stationary, social media stickers, and Google Meet backgrounds. Focused on creating versatile and professional assets to support both digital and print needs.

  • Social Media Visuals

    Conducted extensive visual research to establish a consistent and clean style for social media posts. Created templates to simplify post creation and maintain brand consistency.

  • Employee Welcome Style

    Revamped the employee welcome image from a playful concept to a more professional and minimal style. This change aimed to improve employee engagement and encourage sharing on professional networks like LinkedIn.

  • Website Update

    Implemented a significant update to the company website, enhancing its functionality and visual appeal. Created a dedicated page to document these changes and provide an overview of the improvements made. More info about website update.
