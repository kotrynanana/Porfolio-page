I'm Kotryna Brazaityte, a versatile designer and developer with a Bachelor's degree in Printing and Publishing from Kauno Kolegija. With a strong foundation in print design and a growing passion for web design, I specialize in branding, UI/UX, and front-end development.

My recent work includes roles at IMPACT BRANDS where I focused on visual brand building and post-production projects.

Certified in JavaScript Full-Stack and with skills in the MERN stack, SASS, Figma, and more, I blend traditional creativity with modern digital solutions.
